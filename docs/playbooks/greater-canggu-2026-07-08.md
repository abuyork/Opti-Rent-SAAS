# Greater Canggu Market Playbook — 2026-07-08

_176 entire-home listings sampled across 5 bedroom cohorts (top and bottom of the TTM RevPAR distribution, ≥90 available days, RevPAR > 0). Viral score = within-cohort percentile blend: 50% RevPAR, 30% occupancy, 20% review quality, +5 Guest Favorite._

## Top actions (highest leverage first)

1. Write and publish a full 2,600+ char description this week using a named-villa opener, a design/architect story, a scannable 'What to expect:' bullet block, and walkability times — the biggest single gap since nearly every loser has an empty description.
2. Reshoot the cover as a bright daytime, people-free frame with a clean turquoise pool in the lower third and the designed facade/pergola behind it (mirror winners 92.1, 97.6, 94.1); kill any text overlays, flower-message drone shots, or bare-room interiors.
3. Raise photo count to the winner median for your size (30 for 1BR, 43 for 2BR, 40 for 3BR, 60+ for 4BR, 120+ for 5+BR); losers cluster at 10-20 photos.
4. Rewrite the title to ~45-50 chars stacking a style word (Mediterranean/Tropical/Chic/Romantic/Luxury) + a hero feature (jacuzzi/sauna/rooftop/2 pools/beach) + area, e.g. replace 'ritha villa canggu' with '2BR Tropical Chic Villa w/ Pool near Canggu Beach'.
5. Add and list the high-delta amenities you're missing — outdoor shower, full toiletry set (shampoo/conditioner/body soap/shower gel), hair dryer, wine glasses, dining table, dishes/silverware — and tag 'Pool view'/'Garden view' explicitly.
6. Earn Superhost and pursue Guest Favorite status: winner cohorts are ~89% Superhost / ~55% Guest Favorite while losers are near 0% — this correlates with the entire occupancy gap.
7. Reposition on price toward the cohort winner ADR rather than discounting; winners hold 3-6x loser rates at 64-75% occupancy, so under-pricing a bare listing (loser pattern) does not buy bookings.
8. Turn OFF reliance on Instant Book as a fix and instead invest in design staging (lime-plaster/arch/pergola aesthetic, styled loungers with towels) since 0% of top 1BR/3BR winners use Instant Book while losers over-index on it.

## Cover photos

- **Winners lead with a bright, empty-of-people daytime hero shot where a clean turquoise/green pool anchors the lower third and a designed facade or open-plan interior fills the frame; losers lead with either cluttered, dim interiors or gimmicky staging.**
  - Evidence: All 10 winner covers (e.g. 1BR 92.1 Mediterranean pool, 2BR 97.6 rooftop-jacuzzi villa, 2BR 94.1 desert-oasis) center a crisp pool in blue sky/daylight. Loser 2BR 'ritha villa' (26.1) shows a bare grey sofa + water dispenser under a spotlit wall; loser 2BR 'Creative Home Studio' (25) shows a dim dining table with a fake ficus and a cat tower.
- **Winners use a coherent design signature — Mediterranean/Mykonos white lime-plaster, arches, terracotta or pink walls, bleng-wood pergolas and rattan — that reads instantly as 'designer villa'; losers show generic dated interiors (varnished wood floors, alang-alang roofs, IKEA-grade furniture).**
  - Evidence: Winner 1BR 86.7 has a white arched Santorini-style facade with freestanding tub; winner 1BR 87.2 has a pink Moroccan bedroom with woven ceiling. Loser 1BR 'Charming 1br' (19.3) is a bare varnished-floor bedroom with a wall-mounted TV and generic art; loser 1BR 'Stunning 1BR' (17.9) is a plain white apartment lounge.
- **Winners never put text overlays or literal props on the cover; losers use gimmick/text staging that cheapens the frame.**
  - Evidence: Loser 1BR 'Private Pool Villa Canggu' (20.3) cover is a drone shot of a 'HAPPY HONEYMOON' flower arrangement on decking — no architecture visible; loser 1BR 'Stunning 1BR' (17.9) shows a TV displaying the NETFLIX logo. No winner cover contains text or branded screens.
- **Winners stage the outdoor lounge as a lifestyle scene — plumped white/striped cushions, sun loungers with rolled towels, umbrellas, styled coffee tables; losers show unstyled or empty furniture and harsh mixed lighting.**
  - Evidence: Winner 2BR 97.6 has cream chaise loungers + umbrella; winner 1BR 82.2 (Villa Ratu) has crisp green-striped daybeds with towels. Loser 2BR 25 has washed-out backlit curtains and a random cat-tree; loser 2BR 26.1 mixes daylight window glare with a warm spotlight, flattening the room.
- **A dusk/blue-hour interior with warm lamp glow works when the design is strong, but is the exception — winners default to daylight blue-sky exteriors.**
  - Evidence: Only winner 1BR 81.9 uses a dusk shot (purple sky through glass, lit raffia chandelier, sunset on the TV); the other 9 winners are bright daytime pool-forward frames.

  - Evidence: Edges: 3BR pool view +0.75 (75% W, 0% L), garden view +0.625; 2BR pool view +0.56; 5+BR pool view +0.625. Many losers list 'Pool' but not 'Pool view'.

## Pricing

- **Winners command 3-6x the ADR of losers AND still fill the calendar — this is a quality/positioning gap, not a discount game.**
  - Evidence: Median ADR (IDR): 1BR 4.03M (W) vs 0.74M (L); 2BR 5.15M vs 1.16M; 3BR 9.05M vs 2.74M; 5+BR 29.2M vs 4.87M. Winners sustain ~0.64-0.73 occupancy while losers sit near 0.003.
- **RevPAR gap is driven by occupancy, not just rate — losers are effectively unsold inventory.**
  - Evidence: Median RevPAR: 1BR 2.74M (W) vs 1,884 (L); 4BR 6.40M vs 12,377; 5+BR 16.8M vs 24,329. Winner occupancy ~64-75%; loser occupancy ~0.3%.
- **Winners do NOT rely on Instant Book; losers over-index on it, suggesting it does not rescue weak listings.**
  - Evidence: Instant_book_share: 1BR 0% (W) vs 22% (L); 3BR 0% vs 37.5%; 5+BR 12.5% vs 37.5%.
- **Explicit promo/discount framing appears among high earners only when paired with premium scale — it is a lever, not a crutch.**
  - Evidence: Winner titles '30% Off! 14Bed...' (5+BR 86.6) and 'PROMO Luxury 4bd...w/ Huge Pvt Pool' (4BR 91.3) still post ADRs of 44.6M and 7.8M IDR respectively; discounting on a bare listing (losers) does nothing for occupancy.
- **Winners keep sensible minimum-night floors matched to size (2 nights small, 3 for 4BR+); some losers set higher min-nights on unsold larger units.**
  - Evidence: Median min_nights: 4BR 3 (W) vs 4 (L); 1BR/2BR/3BR winners at 2. Loser 4BR cohort sits at 4-night minimum with ~0 occupancy.
